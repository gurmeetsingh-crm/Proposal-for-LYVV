Brevo Automation Detail

11 automated workflows — built for LYVV

Each workflow maps to a real user or business behavior. No generic templates.

01
Review Nudge
Trigger: User visits a place, no review after 10 days
Personalized email with the place they visited — "How was your experience at [Place]? Share a quick review."
02
Welcome Series
Trigger: New app sign-up
3-email sequence: Day 1 — explore Dine. Day 3 — discover Stay. Day 5 — try Experiences. Guides them through the full app.
03
Win-Back
Trigger: User inactive for 30+ days
"Here's what you've been missing" — trending spots, new listings, and upcoming events in their city.
04
Business Lead Nurture
Trigger: New business enters Pipedrive pipeline
4-email sequence showing LYVV's value: app reach, review visibility, featured placement, success stories from other businesses.
05
Weekly Digest
Trigger: Every Monday for active users
Curated weekly email with top-rated places, new additions, and events — mirrors the #ThisWeek section from the app.
06
Milestone Celebration
Trigger: User hits 5th, 10th, 25th review or ranks up on leaderboard
Congratulatory email with their stats, leaderboard position, and a nudge to keep going. Gamification via email.
07
Referral Prompt
Trigger: User leaves a 4 or 5 star review
Strike while they're happy — "Loved this place? Invite a friend to LYVV." Includes shareable link or code.
08
Post-Experience Follow-up
Trigger: User books an experience or visits a Stay
48-hour follow-up asking for a review + recommending similar places. Closes the feedback loop.
09
New in Your Area
Trigger: New restaurant/hotel/experience listed in user's city
Location-based notification email — "A new [restaurant] just joined LYVV near you." Drives discovery and first visits.
10
Business Onboarding
Trigger: Deal marked "won" in Pipedrive
Welcome the new partner business — how to optimize their listing, respond to reviews, track performance on LYVV.
11
Churned Business Re-engage
Trigger: Listed business inactive on platform for 60+ days
Re-engagement sequence for businesses not updating their listing — share engagement data, tips to improve visibility.
